FAQ/Activities


What is the best time of the year to go house boating?

It’s always a good time to go houseboating! Summer (between Memorial Day and Labor Day) is the most popular and is the busiest season. However, fall offers Indian Summer days with cooler nights.

What is the age requirement to rent a house boat?

The renter must be at least 21 years of age with a valid drivers’ license.

How early do I need to reserve?

Generally, people reserve about 3-6 months in advance. But it’s always a good idea to make a reservation as soon as you’re ready, especially if you have specific dates in mind or if you’re planning a trip over a holiday weekend or on a special promotion.

How do I get there?
